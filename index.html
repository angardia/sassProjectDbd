Dead by Daylight death is not an escape

Dead by Daylight is an asymmetrical multiplayer horror game where one player takes on the role of a brutal Killer and the other four play as Survivors.

As a Killer, your goal is to sacrifice as many Survivors as possible. As a Survivor, your goal is to escape and avoid being caught and killed.

Add-ons

Add-ons boost specific aspect of a Killer's Power and a Survivor's Item when equipped.
Add-ons are an addition to Killer Powers and Survivor Items

Perks

Perks are a type of Unlockables and a core Game Mechanic.
Perks can be unlocked by both Killers and Survivors to grant the chosen Character special abilities in the Trials.

Offerings

There are currently 7 rarities of Offerings featured in Dead by Daylight.
They are used to influence The Entity in how it will shape the Maps and/or grant a special ability or reward.

The Realms

The Realms are semi-procedural locations within the World of The Entity. Each Realm was the site of a violent tragedy in the past and they now serve as the Arenas for The Entity's trials.
